I am currently a third-year PhD student at the Computer Vision Research Group, University of Amsterdam, supervised by Dr. Shaodi You and Prof. Theo Gevers. My current research interest lies in robust vision in bad weather, domain generalization and illumination invariance priors.

Before that, I got my bacholar and master degree from Wuhan University on 2017 and 2020 respectively, supervised by Prof. Kun Qin and Prof. Gui-song Xia, with a strong research interest on aerial image representation learning and multiple instance learning.

In the early 2020, I served as a research intern at the Tencent Jarvis Lab, led by Dr. Yefeng Zheng, focusing on retinal image understanding.

I serve as a regular reviewer for multiple highly-impacted journals including IJCV, TIP, TGRS, and for multiple top-tier conferences including CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ECCV, MICCAI.
