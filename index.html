Biography

He is currently a third-year PhD student at the Computer Vision Research Group, University of Amsterdam, supervised by Dr. Shaodi You and Prof. Theo Gevers. His current research focus on robust vision in bad weather, domain generalization and self-supervised learning.

Before that, He got his bacholar (BEng) and master degree (MSc) from Wuhan University in 2017 and 2020, supervised by Prof. Kun Qin and Prof. Gui-song Xia, with a research focus on aerial image representation learning and multiple instance learning.

In early 2020, He did research internship at the Tencent Jarvis Lab, led by Dr. Yefeng Zheng, focusing on retinal image understanding.
