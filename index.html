Biography

He is currently a third-year PhD student at the Computer Vision Research Group, University of Amsterdam, supervised by Dr. Shaodi You and Prof. Theo Gevers. His current research interest lies in robust vision in bad weather, domain generalization and self-supervised learning.

Before that, He got his bacholar (BEng) and master degree (MSc) from Wuhan University on 2017 and 2020 respectively, supervised by Prof. Kun Qin and Prof. Gui-song Xia, with a strong research interest on aerial image representation learning and multiple instance learning.

In the early 2020, He did research internship at the Tencent Jarvis Lab, led by Dr. Yefeng Zheng, focusing on retinal image understanding.

Honors and Awards

He was awarded as an outstanding reviewer for CVPR2023 (top 3.3%).

His work multi-rater medical image segmentation was shortlisted for CVPR2021 best paper nomination.

His work multi-instance medical image recognition was shortlisted for MICCAI2021 Young Scientist Award nomination.

Multiple his journal publications on aerial scene representation learning have been indexed by Essential Science Indicators (ESI) as higly cited paper.

In 2019, he was awarded as National Excellent Graduate Students by Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China.
